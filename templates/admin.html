Football Manager players

The squad pool players draft from in Team Manager. Upload a CSV to replace the built-in list with your own. FPL-standard pricing: £100m squad budget, prices £4.0–£14.0m.

Columns: name, short_name, club, country, positions, rating, price, age. Positions can be multiple, e.g. MID/FWD. Leave price blank to auto-derive from rating. You need at least 2 GK, 6 DEF, 6 MID, 4 FWD.

Bulk add

Paste many items at once, one per line, using | (pipe) to separate fields. Click Preview to check before adding.

Format: category | question | correct answer | wrong1 | wrong2 | wrong3
Categories: football, geography, science, movies, music, history, pop
Example: science | What is the largest planet? | Jupiter | Mars | Earth | Venus
Format: emoji | answer | category
Example: 🌙🚶 | moonwalk | word
